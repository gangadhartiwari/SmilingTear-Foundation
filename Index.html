About Us

Dedicated to creating lasting positive change in communities across India

Our Story

The Smiling Tear Foundation was born from a simple belief: that every child deserves access to quality education and healthcare, regardless of their economic background.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between privilege and poverty, we work tirelessly to ensure that no dream remains unfulfilled due to lack of resources.

Today, we're proud to have impacted thousands of lives through our various programs and initiatives.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has equal access to education, healthcare, and opportunities for growth.

Our Mission

Empowering communities through sustainable programs in education, healthcare, and skill development.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, transparency, and unwavering commitment to social change.

Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to create lasting impact

Education & Scholarship Program

Providing comprehensive educational support including:

  • Free tutoring and mentorship for underprivileged students
  • Scholarship programs for meritorious students
  • Learning materials and digital resources
  • Career guidance and skill development workshops

Healthcare & Wellness

Ensuring accessible healthcare through:

  • Regular health camps in rural areas
  • Free medical consultations and basic treatments
  • Awareness programs on hygiene and nutrition
  • Medicine distribution to needy patients

Skill Development

Empowering communities with:

  • Vocational training programs
  • Women empowerment initiatives
  • Livelihood generation projects
  • Digital literacy programs
